1. Sign Up

Fill up the forms. Your password and your friends' ethereum address.

2. Login

Put your correct password. When you login correctly, the logout button will show up. Your login is valid in 1 hour. After 1 hour, you have to login again.

3. Logout

Click the logout button, and then you will logout

4. Forgot password

When you forgot your password, click the "Forgot password" button, and then 3 reset numbers will be generated, which only your friends can see. So ask them to give you the reset numbers.

5. Get Reset Number

When you are asked to get reset number, you can put their address and you can get their reset number. Let them know the number.

6. Reset Password

Input reset numbers you asked to your friends. If 2 of 3 are correct, you can change password. Do not put 0 into the form.
